01 — About

I'm a senior technical product owner and AI/data leader who likes living where the business problem meets the system architecture.

At Swisscom I've spent 3+ years as a Product Owner, owning the vision, strategy and roadmap for three conversational-AI products from concept to launch across two cross-functional teams. Today I'm a Staff Data Engineer and Deputy Product Owner for Conversational AI, GenAI & Analytics — pairing product strategy with hands-on engineering, comfortable in any conversation about data models, APIs or LLM pipelines.

Before product, I was Director of Speech Processing R&D, partnering with Microsoft Research, EPFL/Idiap and the University of Zurich. I hold a PhD in speech processing & machine learning, have authored 40+ peer-reviewed publications, and am SAFe® 5 PO/PM certified.
